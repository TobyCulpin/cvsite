Other qualifications

The Sixth Term Exam Papers (STEP) are used by the Universities of Cambridge and Warwick to test their best applicants in their mathematical proficiency. They use the content of Maths A level, but the challenges are far more in depth, more similar to University style questions.

I undertook a 3 week accelerated software course this September, run by Code Nation.

Kimberley STEM College

I fell in love with mathematics during my time at Kimberley. I put myself forward for clubs and events such as Pi day where we went to a lower school to introduce them to concepts of coding using the Rasberry Pi. Also I joined Rocket club, debate society, and partook in serveral maths challenges such as the UKMC senior kangeroo.
